# Daily Operations Checklist

**Project**: NexusPOS AI
**Phase**: Pilot Launch

Print this checklist and keep it near the POS terminal to ensure smooth opening and closing procedures.

## Morning (Opening) Checklist

- [ ] **Turn on Devices**: Power on the POS terminal and the Kitchen Tablet (KDS).
- [ ] **Verify Connectivity**: Ensure both devices are connected to the designated business Wi-Fi network.
- [ ] **Printer Check**: Ensure the thermal printer is powered on and has sufficient paper roll. Print a test receipt if necessary.
- [ ] **Log In (Front)**: Cashier logs into the POS terminal.
- [ ] **Log In (Kitchen)**: Kitchen staff logs into the KDS tablet.
- [ ] **Clear Old KDS Tickets**: Check the KDS screen to ensure there are no lingering tickets from the previous night. If there are, bump them to "SERVED" to clear the board.
- [ ] **Check Inventory Alerts**: Manager logs in and checks the **Inventory** dashboard for any low-stock alerts.

## Afternoon (Mid-Day) Checklist

- [ ] **Tablet Battery Check**: Ensure the KDS tablet remains plugged into its charger.
- [ ] **Receipt Paper Check**: Monitor the thermal printer and replace the paper roll before the dinner rush if it is running low (look for the pink/red stripe on the paper).
- [ ] **Wipe Down Screens**: Use a dry microfiber cloth to wipe grease and moisture off the KDS tablet screen to maintain touch sensitivity.

## Evening (Closing) Checklist

- [ ] **Process Final Orders**: Ensure all pending orders are completed on the POS and bumped off the KDS screen.
- [ ] **Reconcile Cash**: Manager navigates to the **Analytics Dashboard**. Note the total under the "CASH" payment breakdown. Count the physical cash drawer to ensure it matches this number perfectly.
- [ ] **Log Out**: Log out of all accounts on the POS and KDS to prevent unauthorized access overnight.
- [ ] **Power Down**: Turn off the thermal printer. Do NOT turn off the POS terminal if it is configured to receive automated remote software updates.
