# Quick Start Guide (2-Page Cheat Sheet)

Welcome to NexusPOS AI! This guide is designed to get your restaurant up and running in 15 minutes.

---

## Page 1: First-Time Setup (Owner)

### 1. Log In & Brand Your Store
*   Go to your NexusPOS URL and log in with your Admin credentials.
*   Click **Settings > Business Settings**.
*   Enter your **Store Name** and upload your **Logo**. Click **Save**.

### 2. Set Up Taxes & Receipts
*   Go to **Settings > Taxes**. Set your standard GST/VAT percentage.
*   Go to **Settings > Receipts**. Type a "Thank You" message for your footer.

### 3. Add Your First Menu Item
*   Go to **Menu > Categories**. Create a category named "Drinks".
*   Go to **Menu > Items**. Click **Create Item**.
*   Name it "Water Bottle", set the price to $2.00, assign it to "Drinks", and hit **Save**.

**You are now ready to take orders!**

---

## Page 2: Taking Orders (Cashier & Kitchen)

### 1. Take an Order (POS)
1. Click **POS** on the left menu.
2. Ensure you are on **DINE-IN** and type Table "1".
3. Tap "Water Bottle" to add it to the cart.
4. The customer hands you a $5 bill. Tap **CASH** under the payment section.
5. Enter `$2.00` in the amount box. Click **Add Payment**.
6. The balance is zero. Click **Complete Order**.

### 2. Prepare the Order (KDS)
1. On your kitchen tablet, open the **KDS** module.
2. A ticket for "Table 1 - Water Bottle" will instantly appear (Status: **NEW**).
3. Tap it once. Status changes to **PREPARING**.
4. Tap it again. Status changes to **READY**.
5. Once the waiter takes the water bottle to the table, tap it one last time. Status changes to **SERVED** and disappears from the screen.
