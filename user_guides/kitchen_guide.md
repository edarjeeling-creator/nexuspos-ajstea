# Kitchen Staff User Guide

Welcome to the Kitchen Display System (KDS). This screen replaces paper tickets and helps you organize your orders efficiently.

## 1. Accessing the KDS
1. Navigate to the **KDS** module from the main sidebar.
2. If your restaurant has multiple stations (e.g., KITCHEN, BAR, DESSERT), select your designated station from the dropdown. You will only see items routed to your specific station.

## 2. Reading a Ticket
*   **Header**: Displays the Order Number (e.g., #1024) and the Order Type (Dine-In, Takeaway, Delivery).
*   **Timer**: Displays exactly how long the ticket has been open.
    *   *Green*: Less than 5 minutes.
    *   *Yellow*: 5 to 10 minutes.
    *   *Red*: Over 10 minutes (Priority).
*   **Table/Token**: Identifies where the food should go once prepared.
*   **Items**: The list of dishes to prepare.
*   **Notes**: Any special instructions from the cashier will be highlighted in yellow (e.g., "No Onions").

## 3. Workflow Steps
As you work on tickets, update their status to keep the front-of-house staff informed:

1.  **NEW**: The order just arrived from the POS.
2.  **PREPARING**: Tap the ticket to move it to Preparing. This signals the cashier you have started working on it.
3.  **READY**: Tap the ticket again when the food is plated and ready for pickup.
4.  **BUMP (SERVED)**: Tap the ticket one final time when the server has picked up the food. The ticket will instantly disappear from your screen, keeping your board clean.

## 4. Audio Alerts
Ensure the volume on your tablet/display is turned up. A notification chime will play whenever a new order is sent from the POS.
