# Owner & Administrator Guide

Welcome to NexusPOS! As an Owner or Admin, you have full control over your restaurant operations, inventory, and analytics.

## 1. Initial Store Setup
1. Navigate to **Settings -> Business Settings**.
2. Enter your Legal Entity Name, GSTIN/Tax ID, Address, and Contact Details.
3. Upload your Store Logo. This logo will appear on the POS screen and printed receipts.
4. Navigate to **Settings -> Tax Settings** to define your default tax rates for Dine-In, Takeaway, and Delivery.

## 2. Managing the Menu & Inventory
* **Categories**: Start by creating categories (e.g., "Beverages", "Mains") in `Menu -> Categories`.
* **Raw Ingredients**: Navigate to `Inventory -> Items` to add raw ingredients (e.g., Coffee Beans, Milk).
* **Menu Items**: Create sellable items in `Menu -> Items`. Assign them to categories and set their prices.
* **Recipes**: To track theoretical inventory consumption automatically, link a Recipe to a Menu Item. Define exactly how many grams/ml of raw ingredients are used when a Menu Item is sold.

## 3. Analytics & Reporting
1. Navigate to the **Analytics Dashboard** via the sidebar.
2. View real-time **Today's Sales**, **Weekly Trends**, and **Payment Method Breakdowns**.
3. Use the **Top Sellers** chart to determine which menu items to promote and which to drop.
